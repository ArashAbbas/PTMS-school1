ABOUT US

Welcome to Pacific Trails Middle School! We are located in the Pacific Highlands Ranch neighborhood in Carmel Valley, next to Canyon Crest Academy. Pacific Trails is a proud member of San Dieguito Union High School District, which, stands out as a model of public school success. We have received many local, state and national accolades, honors and awards in our long history.
